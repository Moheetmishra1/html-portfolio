My Top 3 movies of all time

1.Zindagi na Milegi dobara

The movie falls into the drama and adventure genres and explores themes of friendship,
self-discovery, and living life to the fullest. If you enjoy movies that delve into these themes, you might have been drawn to it.

2.Lucy

Unlocking human potential: "Lucy" delves into the idea that humans only use a small
fraction of their brain capacity, and by unlocking the full potential of the human mind, one can achieve extraordinary abilities. This concept encourages viewers to ponder the untapped potential of the human brain and the possibilities of enhancing cognitive capabilities.

3 Idiots

One of the central themes of the movie is the importance of pursuing one's passion and
interests. The film encourages viewers to choose a career that aligns with their true calling rather than succumbing to societal pressure or following the conventional path.
